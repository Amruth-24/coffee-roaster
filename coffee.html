Sunset Roasters

Welcome to Sunset Roasters, a small-batch coffee roastery and cafe. We roast single-origin beans, craft signature espresso drinks, and teach barista classes. Our head barista is known as the Sunset Barista.

Our Story

Founded in 2015 by three friends who loved espresso and travel. We started as a pop-up on the pier and grew into a cozy storefront with warm lights, lattes, and live music on Sundays.

Menu Highlights

  • Sunset Blend — medium roast, chocolate & caramel notes
  • Espresso — single shot or double
  • Signature: Sunset Latte — cinnamon and orange zest
  • Cold Brew — 24-hour steep

Classes & Events

Barista 101, Latte Art workshop, Roasting Basics. Next big event: Summer Espresso Festival 2025.
